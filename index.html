About me:

My name is Jiaxiang. I am a Youtuber, coder, and engineer that loves to make and tinker with electronics. Everything with electronics actually started in "Chinese school". Apart from taking Chinese, there are other classes, like Chess, Math, and of course, Robotics. It was my mom who signed me up for it, just becuase I liked making functional toys out of cardboard. That Robotics class changed my life forever. Now I have 2 3D printers, one of which I donated to my former school, Louise Archer.
